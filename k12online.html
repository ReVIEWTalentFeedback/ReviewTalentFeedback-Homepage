Hybrid and Blended Coaching

Meeting the Needs of Today's K12 Online Teacher

Our tools are designed to put the emphasis on evidence driven feedback. To meet this goal we provide K12 online schools the flexibility for both live and video based observation. Teachers can upload video artifacts. They can record screencasts of their teaching and share it with their evaluators.

Coaches and facilitators can embed videos into forms. Online faculty can receive video based feedback on their instructional design.

The first step is to define what better online instruction looks like at your school. Our coaches wil work with your faculty to develop a framework for effective online instruction. We begin with Open Source tools such as the Department of Education's Future Ready Tools for Personalized Learning and Mozilla's Web Literacy Map. We then apply research of effective online teaching and develop tools your K12 online school can use to evaluate and train online teachers.

Our team consists of the most highly respected members of field. We will develop customizable tutorials and plans for your online instructors. You can schedule video based and on the ground coaching and instructional design settings.

Observing K12 Online Instructors

Providing Strategies for Observing Online Instruction

We know teaacher quality matters. In fact teachers have one of thge largest impacts on student learnign gains after controlling for variables beyond a schools control. Yet little is known about how to observe K12 online teachers.

We will work with you to develop an observation plan. Your teachers will use their feedback to drive improvement knowing that their evaluators have received extensive training and have proven their proficiency at writing feedback reports.
