Industry Experts Team Up To Rethink Teacher Evaluation

For Immediate Release: May 1, 2016

Patrick Flynn of ReVision Learning Partnership , LLC. and J. Gregory Mcverry, Ph.D. have joined together to help reshape teacher evaluation. As part of these efforts the two have created a new platform to drive evidence based feedback, ReVIEW Talent Feedback System. As recognized industry leaders the two founders hope to use their knowledge to drive instructional capacity by providing a tool centered on feedback and growth.

The REVIEW Talent Feedback System is a web-based solution offering multiple professional learning applications for schools, districts and state educational organizations. Each application offers professional learning and development that is the direct result of high quality feedback to educators to ensure continuous improvement in performance. Feedback for growth that can be routinely measured, supported and developed is the central solution provided by the ReVIEW Talent Feedback System.

ReVIEW Talent Feedback System Finds Traction in Connecticut

For Immediate Release: May 14, 2016

Earlier this week ReVIEW Talent Feedback System signed on two additional districts in Connecticut to its new platfrom for teacher evaluation. Ledyard Public Schools and Trumball Public Schools will be joining Killingly and Vernon as districts new to the ReVIEW platform in the month of May.

Ledyard and Trumball Public Schools will take adavantage of ReVIEW Talent Feedback System's innovative video based calibration activities. This service allows districts to have their evaluators and new administrators write anbd score observation reports against a set of normed videos. The evaluators reports are then scored against the ReVision Learing Supervisory Continuum. The evaluators also get a comprhensive narrativ ereport analyzing the feedback they give teachers.

Patrick Flynn, co-founder of ReVIEW Talent Feedback System notes, "No one does calibration like us. We use what three decades of research ino learning has taught us, feedback matters. We provide evaluators with feedback to drive their own growth in capacity."

Th growth in districts flocking to ReVIEW Talent Feedback System is also due to its ease of use as a platform. Killingly will be using ReVIEW as a comprehensive approach to tracking teacher feedback and growth. Vernon Public Schools will focus on the instructional capacity and talent management of administrators. School systems find ReVIEW Talent Feedback System as a liberating interface that helps return control from the compliance driven systems that have come to dominate the market place. The plafrom includes customizable forms, teacher reflections, and flexible frameworks.

Greg McVerry notes that it may be ReVIEW customer driven approach that is driving their growth, "For many years the little guy felt lost in a complex system of evaluation. They want the flexibility to shape their evaluation plan driven by local needs not the limitations of the tools they use."

ReVIEW Talent Feedback System Establishes Parterships Idustry Wide Partnerships

For Immediate Release: May 25, 2016

As a mission driven company ReVIEW Talent Feedback System strives to help districts empower teachers through talent management. In pursuit of these efforts ReVIEW Talent Feedback System has established strategic partnerships with The BERC Group and ReVision Learnign Partnership.

As part of the partnership with The BERC Group ReVIEW Talent Feedback System will license videos from their professional development series. States and districts can then have these videos normed against their chosen frameworks by ReVIEW's expert team. These videos can than be used as part of calibration trainings with new administrators and evaluators. ReVIEW Talent Feedback receives the scored reports from district evaluators. They then score the report against the ReVision Supervisory Learning Continuum and write their own detailed report for the district evaluator. Basically a ReVIEW Talent feedback coach provides you with the district administrators with the smae type of feedback we expect them to write for teachers.

ReVIEW Talent Feedback System has also established a parntership with ReVision Learning Partership. ReVision is an industry leader in the field of teacher evaluation. They provide servcies to over forty school districts across the country. As part of the agreement ReVIEW Talent Feedback Ssyem licenses the ReVision Supversiory Learning Continuum and ReVision can use the ReVIEW Talent Feedback system platform with their clients.

"I feel like what ReVIEW brings was a major missing link to evaluation systems," notes Amy Tepper, a senior ReVision Learning Partnership. She continued, "Not every district has the means to support our intensive on the ground training. The ReVIEW Talent Fedback System gives us the opportunity to provide hybrid coaching."

In fact the ReVIEW Talent Feedback System's API driven platform allows for easy integration with partners and more teams will surely be announced soon.

Flexible Frameworks

Returning District Level Control

ReVIEW Talent Feedback System will installed with your state approved frameworks. However we empower districts be giving admin rights to change and modify frameworks and assign frameworks by positions. ReVIEW also comes installed with the Revision Learning Continuum to allow your district to improve evaluator capacity.

Flexible Forms

Adapting to your Teacher Evaluation Plan

ReVIEW Talent Feedback System's form builder provides every district the opportunity to customize the system to meet their needs. You can use our pre-populated forms or create your own.

We have the majority of frameworks pre-installed but the ReVIEW Talent Feedback System also allows you to upload or customize any rubric or framework used in your district.

Educator Preparation

Engineering Better Teachers

ReVIEW Talent Feedback System is designed with the goal of improving teacher preparation. Schools of Education in rural communities use ReVIEW's video upload to observe remote teachers. Candidates across the country find value in being scored using the same frameworks they will use in the classroom.

The ReVIEW Talent Management System is the only comprehensive solution for States who wish to track teacher candidates beyond student teaching.

We offer training for educator and calibration of your student teaching supervisors. We also offer expert scoring of student observations. Student teachers deserve highly trained and calibrated supervisors. While most universities and colleges use retired teachers and administrators who bring years of experience. Yet as evaluators they receieve little to no training. Using our library of normed videos educator preparation programs offer supervisors professional development while also making sure they have the necessary skillset to evaluate student teachers.
